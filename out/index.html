Durga Tea Company

Brand identity guidelines for 'Durga Tea', a conceptual tea company. This project explores the visual identity, color palettes, typography, and packaging concepts inspired by the strength, serenity, and symbolism associated with the deity Durga. The aim is to create a brand that evokes warmth, power, and tranquility, reflecting the essence of both the tea and its divine namesake.
